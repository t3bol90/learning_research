# Student mindsets that don't suit research

<!-- zh: 1. 羞于求助。不好意思问人问题。 -->
1. Being shy about asking for help. Feeling awkward asking other people questions.
<!-- zh: 2. 过于依靠自己的大脑独立完成事情。被做作业、考试养成的思维习惯。仿佛从网上、向别人找答案就是在作弊。 -->
2. Relying too much on your own brain to finish things on your own. A thinking habit built up by doing homework and sitting exams. As if looking up answers online or asking other people counts as cheating.
<!-- zh: 3. 习惯几周的课程作业这种短期项目。难以从长期科研项目中获取乐趣。 -->
3. Being used to short-term projects, like coursework that lasts a few weeks. Struggling to get any enjoyment out of long-term research projects.
<!-- zh: 4. 习惯被学校、老师安排好自己做事情。等着被布置任务，缺乏自身的规划，遵守规则但从不研究规则，没自己的想法。 -->
4. Being used to having the school and the teacher line up everything for you. Waiting around to be handed a task, with no plan of your own. Following the rules but never studying the rules. Having no ideas of your own.
<!-- zh: 5. 习惯性的内卷。在意他人的评价，总想着比过别人，精神内耗严重。缺少团结协作的习惯。 -->
5. A habit of internal competition. Caring what other people think, always trying to beat the next person, burning a lot of mental energy on it. Not being in the habit of working together.
<!-- zh: 6. 过于服从老师、学长学姐，不敢表达自己的想法。 -->
6. Being too obedient to teachers and senior students, and not daring to say what you actually think.
