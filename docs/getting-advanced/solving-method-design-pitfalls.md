# Failures in the solving-method-design phase and how to avoid them

<!-- zh: 可能遇到的问题： -->
Problems you may run into:

<!-- zh: 1. 想技术的时候，没有蒸馏相关方向已有论文的知识，全靠自己拍脑袋设计pipeline。 -->
1. When you think about the technical approach, you don't distill the knowledge from existing papers in the related area. You design the pipeline entirely from your own gut feeling.

<!-- zh: 2. 日常project相关的论文自己找得不多，主要靠senior advisor给自己找论文。思路不够开阔。导致在"想解决问题的技术"环节陷入了local minima，没有提出更好的技术。 -->
2. You don't dig up many papers related to the project on your own. You mostly rely on your senior advisor to find papers for you. Your thinking stays narrow. This pushes you into a local minimum during the "come up with a technique to solve the problem" step, and you don't put forward a better approach.

	<!-- zh: 如何避免：平时自己多找论文、多看论文。一个标准：在当前project相关方向，自己看的论文数量大于senior advisor看的论文数量。<br>如果没满足这个标准，估计project估计很可能失败。 -->
	How to avoid this: find and read papers on your own as a regular habit. A simple yardstick: in the area related to your current project, the number of papers you have read should be greater than the number your senior advisor has read.<br>If you don't meet this bar, the project will likely fail.

<!-- zh: 3. 缺少与导师、实验室同学的交流，没有找他们讨论自己当前想出的技术，导致在"想解决问题的技术"环节陷入了local minima，没有提出更好的技术。 -->
3. You don't talk enough with your advisor and lab mates. You don't go to them to discuss the technical ideas you've come up with. As a result, you get stuck in a local minimum during the "come up with a technique to solve the problem" step and fail to propose a better approach.

	<!-- zh: 如何避免：平时多找导师、实验室同学交流。一个标准：想出解决问题的idea后，至少要和导师、几个同学聊一次，看看他们对这个idea的看法。<br>如果没满足这个标准，估计project不会很牛逼。 -->
	How to avoid this: talk to your advisor and lab mates often. A simple yardstick: once you have an idea for solving the problem, you should talk it over at least once with your advisor and a few lab mates and see what they think of it.<br>If you don't meet this bar, the project probably won't turn out great.

4. The pipeline in the project has no technical contribution, so you can't write a paper from it. Or, after you do write the paper, reviewers complain that it has no novelty.
