# Why doing a broad, self-directed paper survey matters

<!-- zh: 对自身的好处： -->
Benefits to yourself:

<!-- zh: 1. 搜索论文能提升自身搜索能力。 -->
1. Searching for papers builds your own searching ability.
<!-- zh: 2. 广泛阅读论文能提升自身技术积累和解决问题的能力。 -->
2. Reading widely builds your technical background and your ability to solve problems.
<!-- zh: 3. 自主调研论文能提升自己独立做科研的能力。 -->
3. Investigating papers on your own builds your ability to do research independently.

<!-- zh: 对Project的好处，可以按以下逻辑来推理： -->
Benefits to the project, which you can reason about as follows:

<!-- zh: 导师的帮助一般有三方面： -->
An advisor usually helps in three ways:

<!-- zh: 1. 对问题的理解。 -->
1. Understanding of the problem.
<!-- zh: 2. 一些前沿的技术、概念。 -->
2. Some of the latest techniques and concepts.
<!-- zh: 3. 对具体某篇论文的分析。 -\> 一般才能得到要具体咋做。但导师看的方向太广，导致总是漏一些关键论文。 -->
3. Analysis of a specific paper. This is usually how you figure out what concretely to do. But an advisor's reading covers too many directions, so they always miss some key papers.

<!-- zh: 学生和导师是互补的。学生在特定project上有充足的时间，可以根据导师提到的一些关键词，去搜寻关键论文，然后分享给导师。 -->
The student and the advisor complement each other. The student has plenty of time on a specific project and can take the keywords the advisor mentions, go hunt down the key papers, and share them with the advisor.

<!-- zh: 导师看了以后，产生更多想法，和学生讨论。学生有更多的想法，去找更多有启发性的问题，从而形成正循环。 -->
After the advisor reads them, more ideas come up and they discuss them with the student. The student then has more ideas, goes looking for more questions that spark thinking, and a positive loop forms.
