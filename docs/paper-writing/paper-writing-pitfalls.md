# Problems in the paper-writing phase and how to avoid them

<!-- zh: 可能遇到的问题： -->
Problems you may run into:

<!-- zh: 1. 论文的"第一眼美感"不足。 -->
1. The paper does not look good at first glance.
<!-- zh: 2. 论文Method的文字部分写得不清楚，让人看不懂方法具体怎么做的。 -->
2. The text in the Method section is unclear, so readers cannot work out how the method actually works.
<!-- zh: 3. 论文Introduction的文字部分写得不清楚，让人看不懂论文有什么contributions。 -->
3. The text in the Introduction is unclear, so readers cannot tell what the paper's contributions are.
<!-- zh: 4. 论文Abstract的文字部分写得不清楚，让人看不懂论文有什么contributions。 -->
4. The text in the Abstract is unclear, so readers cannot tell what the paper's contributions are.
<!-- zh: 5. 论文的Experiments不够充分。 -->
5. The Experiments are not thorough enough.
<!-- zh: 6. 论文Experiment的文字部分写得不清楚，没介绍清楚evaluation metrics。 -->
6. The text in the Experiments section is unclear and does not properly describe the evaluation metrics.
