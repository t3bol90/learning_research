# How to practise paper writing

> Document index (GitHub repo): [https://github.com/pengsida/learning_research](https://github.com/pengsida/learning_research)

There are three paths (all of which, at heart, train your ability to summarise and to express ideas logically):

1. Read one paper a day, then write a summary in your own words:
   1. Describe the novelty of the paper in one sentence.
   2. Describe the pipeline of the paper in a few sentences: what was done in step one, step two, step three, and so on.
2. Be able to clearly describe your ideas, contributions, progress, and questions to others on a regular basis.
3. Make presentation slides, and make sure the slides have a clear logical flow.
