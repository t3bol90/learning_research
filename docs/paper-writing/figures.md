# Drawing figures for the paper

See also: [Paper figure template](./figures-template.md).

> **The method figure matters a lot.** The pipeline figure in the method section needs to look different from prior approaches. Otherwise readers walk away thinking the work has no novelty. If the overall pipeline (from input to output) is not particularly novel, highlight the novel module inside the pipeline figure. Another option is to skip the big pipeline figure and only draw a few small figures, but the paper may then feel less polished visually.
>
> **The pipeline figure is not there to make readers understand the method; it is there to highlight the novelty.** The text in the method section is what makes readers understand it.
>
> Good examples: NSFF, KiloNeRF. A counter-example: AniSDF.
